Piano Sing Along

Pianist Bazz Norton and his 160 year-old Collard & Collard grand piano invite you to sample the delights of Piano Kensington bar and restaurant. With a variety of fine wines, and delicious food on offer you are sure to have a thoroughly enjoyable evening in their company. Come along, sing along and have a truly memorable and fun night in Kensington.

White Truffle Season

Arguably the best season for producing the most delicious ingredients, Autumn is here and white truffles are back! We are celebrating all things truffle from 1st – 30th November, with a Jerusalem artichoke and white truffle risotto on the menu, as well as the option to add shaved truffles on every dish from the Restaurant’s ALa Carte menu…how can you say no to that?

Festive Feasting

This Christmas we’re bringing our guests a touch of festive magic, with D&D London’s Fairytales in the City celebration. As part of this our Head Chef, Jeremy Trehout has created a delicious bespoke menu for you to enjoy. Please note our Festive Menu will be available from 14th November until 24th December. The menus are available for up to 10 guests. Full prepayment will be taken to secure the booking with a 48 hour cancellation pol.
